About AI Career Navigator

AI Career Navigator is an AI-powered resume analysis platform that helps students and professionals evaluate their ATS score, identify skill gaps, generate personalized interview questions, discover suitable projects, and receive role-specific career guidance. The platform uses Gemini AI, Flask, PDF processing, and intelligent resume analysis to provide actionable insights for career growth.
